About

One Step Ahead 

  •                  MISSION

    To provide a one-stop solution to the increasing gap between the availability of medical professionals and people in need of help.
    One Step Ahead is a holistic mental well-being web app that targets people who are facing mental health issues. 




  •                   VISION

    To assist individuals in acquiring skills and techniques necessary to live a happier, fuller and more meaningful life. To develop a greater understanding of one's ability to manifest their desires in life.
